Solar Technology

Our training program starts by providing a solid understanding of solar energy and its benefits. You'll learn about the principles of photovoltaic (PV) technology, solar radiation, and how solar panels convert sunlight into electricity. We'll explore different types of solar panels, their efficiency, and their suitability for various applications. Understanding the fundamentals of solar energy will lay the groundwork for your journey into the world of solar installation.

Embedded System Traning

Our training program starts by providing you with a solid foundation in embedded systems. You will learn about the core concepts, architectures, and components that make up embedded systems. From microcontrollers and processors to sensors and actuators, you'll gain a comprehensive understanding of the building blocks of embedded systems. Through practical exercises and hands-on projects, you'll develop the skills to design, develop, and debug embedded systems effectively.
